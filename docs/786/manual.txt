how to use food market software 786
commands :
[1] document  [2] data  [3] market  [4] price [5] codeline  [6] help  [7] exit  [8] calendar  [9] programs

how to change time or date ?
type "calendar" to change date and time

how to use programs ?
[1] document : write your document and save it with "enter"
[2] data : write your data on /   data 1  / until /  data 5 /
[3] market : type your product name and price of your product
[4] calendar : enter your date and time
[5] price : type your price and your product name

how to exit food market 786 software ?
write "exit" and choose" y" if you want to exit or "n" if you want to back
