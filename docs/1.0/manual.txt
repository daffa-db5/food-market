how to use food market software version 1.0

1. how to open apps
   when you want to open apps, type the numbers in front of an app
    Example : 2.Note  = in front of note there are numbers 2, so you should write down 2 numbers in "open"

-USING APPS

1. Market : when entering the market you will see the view of the market after that you enter the product name and product price.

2. Note : Note is an app used to write notes,  you just write down the notes you want to write, and after name it and save.

3. FMN (Food Market News) : FMN is the app to read the news
